Introduction:

This game is developed in Unreal Engine 5. This is a demo of online multiplayer shooter game represents implemention of multiplayer game mechanics such as creating and joining sessions via steam platform, character movement and advance animation, multiple weapon system including ammo pickups, health pickups to help player survive in the session. It has interactive widget that display health, ammo counts and number of grenades. It includes functionality of apply and take damage, death functionality and respawing again once player is dead. It has a variety of particle system including Niagara system and sound effects to make it feel more adventurous.
